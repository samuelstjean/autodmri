Welcome to autodmri's documentation!
====================================

This is the documentation detailing the internal of Automated characterization of noise distributions in diffusion MRI data, which is available at https://github.com/samuelstjean/autodmri.

You can find the original paper and full details of the algorithm as presented in
::
   St-Jean, Samuel, De Luca, Alberto, Tax, Chantal M. W., Viergever, Max A. and Leemans, Alexander.
   ‘Automated characterization of noise distributions in diffusion MRI data’ (2019)
   Bioarxiv. doi: 10.1101/686436.

Which you can grab a copy from arxiv_.

There is also the previous conference manuscript which details a different set of experiments
::
   St-Jean, Samuel, De Luca, Alberto, Viergever, Max A. and Leemans, Alexander
   ‘Automatic, Fast and Robust Characterization of Noise Distributions for Diffusion MRI’,
   MICCAI 2018. Springer International Publishing, pp. 304–312. doi: 10.1007/978-3-030-00928-1_35.

Which is also available on the publisher website miccai_ or arxiv_.

The datasets are also available at zenodo_.
